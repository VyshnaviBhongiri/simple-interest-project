# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards
**Positive behavior includes:**
- Respectful communication
- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Showing empathy

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any other inappropriate conduct

## Enforcement
Instances of misconduct can be reported to the maintainers at **your-email@example.com**. All issues will be reviewed and addressed fairly.
